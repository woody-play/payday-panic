📖 How to Play
1. Build: Select a tower below, then tap an empty cell to place it.

2. Merge (★): Place 3 identical towers next to each other (up/down/left/right adjacent). They auto-combine into 1 powered-up tower with ×1.5 ammo! Stack up to ★★★.
🐷🐷🐷 → 🐷★ (×1.5 ammo) → keep merging → 🐷★★★ (×3.4 ammo!)
3. Type bonus: Each tower deals 2× damage to a specific expense type (shown as "vs ___" below each tower).

4. Boss: Every 6 months, a mega boss appears. Prepare towers in advance!

5. Flash Sale: Hit enemies to charge the gauge → activate to slash ALL enemies' HP at once.

6. Upgrade: The purple "Upgrade All" button boosts ALL tower damage globally, but also raises building costs.
